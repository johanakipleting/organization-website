Under-Graduate

Ngenytalel University offers a variety of undergraduate programs across different fields of study. The following is a detailed list of the undergraduate courses available:

  • Bachelor of Business Computing

    • This program integrates business principles with computing skills, preparing students for careers in both fields.

  • Bachelor of Library and Information Science

    • Focuses on the management of information resources and services, equipping students with skills necessary for library and information management.

  • Bachelor of Science in Network Systems Administration

    • Students learn about network design, implementation, and maintenance, focusing on systems administration within various organizational contexts.

  • Diploma in Information Technology

    • Aimed at providing foundational IT skills applicable across various industries.

  • Certificate in Information Technology

    • These programs are designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to succeed in their respective fields upon graduation.
