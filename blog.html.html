Our Certificate & Online Programs For 2025

In 2025, several certificate and online programs will be available for individuals seeking to enhance their professional skills and knowledge. These programs are designed to cater to various fields and interests, providing a flexible learning environment that accommodates busy schedules.

This program is newly available and requires applicants to apply by January 21, 2025. All required courses must be completed by the same date to earn the certificate. It is important to note that this program does not substitute for the in-person courses required for the Administrative Assistant Traineeship.
